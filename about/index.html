FoodieFun

Eat. Enjoy. Share!

You like to eat out! We want to know all about it!

How long did you have to wait to be seated? Were the waitstaff friendly and helpful, or rude and dismissive?

You could lug a notebook and take detailed notes each visit - taking your attention away from why you came in the first place (to experience the meal), or you can leverage FoodieFun and easily record and access all the important details, freeing you up to focus exclusively on the meal.
