About Us

FoodieFun

Your Food Adventures...
As told by you

FoodieFun is a platform built for you! We know that there's no place for foodies like you to quickly and easily capture what matters most from your Food Adventures.

Our goal is to help you get ita all - the photos, your impressions, your ratings - all in to one convenient location on the FoodieFun platform.
Using FoodieFun is as simple as ordering from a familiar menu - just add the restaurants you're visiting and the delicious food and drinks you're enjoying while there. Long wait times? No problem - capture every detail with FoodieFun while you wait. Spending quality time and only want to make a quick note? We've got your back - you can always come back to a restaurant or menu item you've added and fill in missing details or edit what's there!
